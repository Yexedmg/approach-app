# Approach

A personal PWA for making approaching girls addicting instead of something you avoid out of fear.

## The Idea

Seeing beautiful girls everywhere and doing nothing is like letting leads go as a business. This app identifies the root cause and fixes it — by making approaching the safer choice than not approaching.

The goal: your chances are not 0% of something fun, but you taking the 10% shot without problems. And never having the feeling that you let go of prospects.

## How It Works

**Stats are like Fortnite stats.** They tell you what already happened — the metrics. Not judgment. Just data.

This app bypasses each psychological step, step by step, from the moment you see her:

- **See her** → instant logging, count goes up
- **Register the encounter** → post-stats screen shows what you won/lost
- **In approach mode** → intention confirmation, opening lines, fear reframes, plan
- **After approaching** → review what happened, improve next time
- **Never approached** → tracked, analyzed, reality-checked

## Why It Works

> "By visualizing and debugging your internal logic, you are engaging in Cognitive Restructuring, which literally rewires your brain through a process called neuroplasticity."

When you see that the "Fear of Approach" is just a broken subroutine — a loop that triggers based on outdated data — you can literally outrun it by moving faster than the lag in your own biological software.

**Goal: make not asking her less beneficial for you internally than yes, asking her.**

## Features

- **GO Mode** — structured approach flow: reason, first line, plan, then go
- **Intention to Approach** — YES/NO commitment gate before GO
- **Post-Encounter Stats** — Win/Loss screen after every miss or approach
- **Your People** — track girls you've interacted with
- **Prospects** — log girls you've seen and are curious about
- **Pretty Girls Seen** — log notable sightings with ratings
- **Opening Lines** — 2 random in approach tab, full page with custom additions
- **Miss Log** — every girl you let go, tracked with reasons
- **Re-encounter Tracker** — did you ever see them again?
- **Stats** — monthly/weekly breakdown, records, Fortnite-style overview
- **Reviews** — post-approach review notes (weakpoints, goodpoints, etc.)
- **Internal** — boldness stat, power level, engagement stat, life's hard stat
- **Feature Ideas** — log features you want built

## Stack

Single-file HTML/CSS/JS PWA. No backend. localStorage. Installable on iOS/Android.
